Welcome to the World of Plants

Photosynthesis

Plants are predominantly photosynthetic eukaryotes of the kingdom Plantae. They play a crucial role in the world's ecosystems by converting sunlight into energy through photosynthesis.

Diversity of Plants

There are about 380,000 known species of plants, ranging from single cells to towering trees. Plants are essential for life as they form the basis of most food webs.

Human Use of Plants

Humans have used plants for food, medicine, and materials for thousands of years. Understanding plants is vital for our future.
